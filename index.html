About Maddy

Built for belonging.

Everyone wants a village but nobody wants to be a villager. Early in life, community forms naturally. It often takes more effort to avoid belonging than to participate. Now that the built-in community is gone, finding my people has become a conscious pursuit.

A sense of community has become a lost language. I am committed to helping keep it alive.

A while back I acted on that instinct. A local restaurant, a Tuesday night, and an idea. What started as one night turned into a series -- and every single time, the room filled up. That didn't surprise me. I've been bringing people together for as long as I can remember. It's not something I do. It's who I am.

What I Do

From concept to full house.

Every business is different. Some need a full event. Some need a great video. Some just need someone who genuinely loves what they do and tells people about it. Here is how I can help.

The Full Experience
Event Production
I build a themed event around your space from the ground up. Concept, promotion, themed menu, execution, hosting -- everything. You open your doors and the room fills up. This is the highest-touch offering and consistently the highest-impact one. Sold-out rooms, doubled bar sales, and guests who keep coming back.
The Content Play
Content and Social Activation
I come in, experience what you offer, and bring my audience with me. Short-form video, authentic storytelling, and organic promotion that feels real because it is. No event required.

Local Brand Partnership
