About Maddy

Built for belonging.

Nobody warns you about this part.

For most of your life, community just happens. You're practically tripping over it. The neighborhood, the team, the lunch table, the job where everyone goes out after. You don't have to try. Belonging is just the water you're swimming in.

Then one day the structure disappears. And suddenly you're getting dressed for something you don't want to go to, standing in a room full of people feeling completely alone, wondering when exactly this got so hard.

Most people assume that's just adulthood. It's not. It's a withdrawal nobody names.

I wanted to see more connection in my community. So I decided to create it.
